how it works

Tell us about your car

Here's what we ask for:
  • License Plate or VIN
  • Trim (LE, SE, SLT, etc)
  • Exact mileage
  • Vehicle condition
  • Vehicle history

Get your price

We'll give you an instant price for your vehicle that is good for 7 days or 250 additional miles (whichever comes first). If we're unable to calculate an instant price, one of our car-buying experts will email you a price typically within the same day.

We pick it up. You get paid.

We’ll schedule a time to come pick up your vehicle, free of charge. Once we have your car, we’ll send your payment.
